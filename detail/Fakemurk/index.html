A set of scripts for spoofing verified while enterprise enrolled. Blends your Chromebook in with others in your organization.

    Instructions

    Enabling Developer Mode
  1. Enter into recovery mode (Esc+Power+Refresh)
  2. Get a recovery image for your board v107 or lower.
  3. Flash the image on a USB drive.
  4. Go into recovery mode and plug in your USB
  5. Once you booted into the image, sign in.
  6. Open crosh by pressing Ctrl+Alt+T
  7. Run shell
  8. Run sudo -i
  9. Run bash <(curl -SLk https://github.com/MercuryWorkshop/fakemurk/releases/latest/download/fakemurk.sh)
  10. Follow the instructions and reboot.
