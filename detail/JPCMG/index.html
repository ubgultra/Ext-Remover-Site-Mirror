chrome://serviceworker-internals and chrome://inspect must not be blocked by policy to perform this exploit.

Alternative method for LTBEEF that utilizes chrome://serviceworker-internals. Compatibility is limited to extensions that have service workers.

    Instructions

  1. Navigate to chrome://serviceworker-internals.
  2. Click the start button under the extension you want to disable.
  3. Click the inspect button under the extension you want to disable.
  4. In the console, execute scripts such as chrome.management.setEnabled('extensionid', false);.
